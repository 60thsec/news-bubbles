Version 0.4.5

11 October 2014

New

  • Links to YouTube videos in comments will be replaced with in-line YouTube players.
  • Hacker News stories now real-time (using the new FireBase API).
  • Links to reply to comments for Hacker News and Reddit.

  • Deleted Reddit comments are showing.
  • Vote buttons show when voting isn't possible.
